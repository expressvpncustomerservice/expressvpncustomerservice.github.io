Step-by-Step: How to reach ExpressVPN support

Follow these simple steps to contact ExpressVPN customer service using the most effective methods:

  1. Visit the official ExpressVPN support page – navigate to the help section. Look for the “Contact Us” or “Support” link (usually in the footer or top menu).

Pro tip: Before contacting support, gather relevant information such as your device type, operating system version, ExpressVPN app version, and a description of the issue. This helps the team assist you faster.

Troubleshooting tips – resolve issues before contacting support

In many cases, you can fix common ExpressVPN issues on your own. Try these troubleshooting steps before reaching out to customer service:

🔹 Slow connection speed? Switch to a different server closer to your location. Use the “Smart Location” feature. Also, try changing your protocol (e.g., from OpenVPN to Lightway) in the settings.

🔹 Can’t connect to a server? Restart the ExpressVPN app, or restart your device. Check your firewall/antivirus settings—they might be blocking the VPN. Try connecting to a different server.

🔹 DNS leaks? Enable the “Network Lock” (kill switch) and ensure your system’s DNS settings are correct. ExpressVPN’s support site has a dedicated DNS leak test guide.

🔹 App crashes or won’t install? Make sure your device meets the minimum system requirements. Clear the app cache, or uninstall and reinstall the latest version from the official website.

🔹 Billing or subscription issues? Log in to your account dashboard to view your plan, payment history, and invoice. Most billing questions can be resolved without contacting support.

🔹 Forgot password? Use the “Forgot password” link on the login page. You’ll receive a reset link via email. If you don’t see it, check your spam folder.

If you’ve tried these and still need help, ExpressVPN’s customer service is just a click away. The support team is known for its professionalism and technical expertise.
